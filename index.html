Typography System

Two Fonts.
One Powerful Brand.

After deep research into your vision — traditional, rooted, Haldiram's-level heritage with global scale — here is the final, definitive font system for Bharati's Kitchen.

Why these exact two fonts?

Your references (Pure Chips, Tifto, Purevite, Bowl'd) all share one thing: their font has visible personality and character — not generic. Fraunces is a "Wonky Old Style" serif — it has organic, ink-pressed warmth like handcrafted Indian signage, but is professional enough for global franchise signage. Plus Jakarta Sans is modern, clean, and reads perfectly on Zomato, Swiggy, Instagram, and packaging labels. Together: heritage + modern.

The Rule

Fraunces for anything big and seen from a distance. Plus Jakarta Sans for anything read up close. Never swap them.

Hindi Text

When writing in Hindi/Devanagari script, use Hind (Google Fonts) — it harmonises perfectly with both fonts.

Never Mix

Only these 2 font families in all brand materials. No Poppins, no Montserrat, no Arial. These two, always.

The 60 / 30 / 10 Rule

60% Cream & Ivory on all backgrounds and large surfaces. 30% Saffron + Forest Green for structure, headers, and key UI elements. 10% Turmeric Gold for highlights, taglines, and accent touches only.

Never: Use Saffron and Gold together in large areas. Never use unauthorised colours. Never pure #000000 or #FFFFFF.

Logo Don'ts

✕
Don't stretch or distort
Always maintain the original proportions. Never scale only width or only height.
✕
Don't use unauthorised colours
Logo only in Saffron, Green, Gold, Cream, Charcoal or pure White. No pink, purple, blue, or red.
✕
Don't place on busy backgrounds
Always place on solid or subtle textured backgrounds. Never on food photos without a clear contrast area.
✕
Don't add effects or shadows
No drop shadows, glows, gradients applied to the logo. No outlines around the letterforms.
✕
Don't use a different font
The wordmark is always Fraunces Bold. Never recreate it in another font — even a similar one.
✕
Don't use below minimum size
Print: 25mm minimum width. Digital: 80px minimum. Favicon: 32px. Below this, use BK mark only.

The Food Integrity Pledge

The future of food is dangerous — chemicals everywhere, shortcuts at every step, artificial in everything. Bharati's Kitchen will always use raw ingredients, real vegetables, and the most honest, healthy raw materials possible.

That will never change — at any scale, in any city, in any country. This is not a marketing line. It is the reason Bharati's Kitchen exists.
